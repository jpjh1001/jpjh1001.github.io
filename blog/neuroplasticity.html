Research Growth Mindset Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

A growth mindset is recognising that your limits are endless. It is realising that our ability and intelligence could be improved through consistent effort, dedication, and learning. It is to recognise failures, challenges, and missed opportunities and use them as a motivator for growth and improvement rather than being frustrated and accepting the limitations of an individual's ability to grow.

It reminded me of the importance of a growth mindset as I believe there is no finish line when it comes to broadening our knowledge. You can never be 'done' studying.

How will you integrate a growth mindset into your learning journey?

Not to have a closed mindset, to stay resilient at all times, and to never give up.
