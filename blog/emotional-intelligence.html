The definition of emotional intelligence says the capacity to be aware of, control, and express one's emotions, and to handle interpersonal relationships judiciously and empathetically. It's the ability to recognise emotinal cues to how other people might be feeling, knowing your own strengths and limits and being empathetic.

IQ is an indicator showing the academic abilities and identifying individuals problem-solving capabilities. EQ is an indicator showing one's ability to control and understand and assess emotions of others and of themselves.

By being able to assess emotions of people, EQ is important as it helps you to analyse situations, build strong relationships and deescalate conflicts which could help you be more socially and emotionally aware.
